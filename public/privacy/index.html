Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This Privacy Policy describes how EventBuddy AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our website and the EventBuddy Discord application. We only describe behaviors present in this codebase and the documented integrations.

1. What information we collect

Depending on how you use EventBuddy, we may process:

  • Discord identifiers: server (guild) ID, channel ID, message ID, and the Discord user ID and username of message authors. These are required to operate features in Discord.
  • Message content: messages posted in channels where the bot is active may be logged to provide AI responses and analytics. The codebase includes utilities that can store message content and AI responses in a database for context and analytics.
  • Event data: event name, date/time, status and related metadata created by server administrators via slash commands.
  • CSV attendee data (optional): if a server admin uploads a CSV to import attendees, we may store fields such as attendee name, email, Discord handle/ID and optional additional columns contained in the CSV. Uploading this data is optional and controlled by the admin.
  • Channel metadata and preferences: channel names/purpose and per-user preferences that help tailor responses and context.
  • Technical data: basic diagnostics and timestamps generated by the application and database (e.g., created_at/updated_at).

2. How we use information

  • Operate the EventBuddy Discord bot and features (slash commands, channel utilities).
  • Generate responses with AI and improve conversation quality and relevance.
  • Provide event and community analytics to server admins.
  • Maintain safety, reliability and troubleshooting of the service.

3. AI processing

EventBuddy integrates with Google Generative AI to produce responses. When the bot needs an AI reply, relevant context (such as recent messages, channel metadata and user preferences) may be sent to the AI provider solely to generate the response. We do not sell your data.

4. Legal bases

  • Contract/necessity: to provide the features you request in Discord.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the service secure, reliable and useful.
  • Consent: for optional features such as attendee CSV uploads by admins.

5. Data sharing

  • Supabase is used for hosting our Postgres database and APIs.
  • Discord provides the platform and APIs where the bot operates.
  • Google Generative AI is used to generate AI responses from provided context.
  • We do not sell personal data. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users.

6. Retention

Records are retained for as long as needed to operate the service. Conversation logs associated with archived events may be cleaned up after approximately 90 days based on the included cleanup routines. Admins can request deletion of specific records subject to platform constraints.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards provided by our infrastructure providers (e.g., Supabase) and limit permissions to what the bot requires. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children

EventBuddy is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the service if you are under the minimum digital consent age in your region.

9. Your choices and rights

  • Server admins can remove the bot, revoke permissions, or request deletion of specific data.
  • You may request access or deletion of data that relates to you by contacting the admin of the server where the bot is installed or by contacting us directly.

11. Changes to this policy
